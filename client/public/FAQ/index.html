What is jchannel?

Jchannel is an online forum where Jessup students and staff can communicate, post comments and share images on a variety of topics. The site was developed purely by a student. Jchannel is not affiliated with any party, but simply serves as an independent communication platform. Users can log in with your school email to enjoy all the features of the site and become part of the community.

How do I login?

Click the menu icon in the top left corner, and then click "My account". You will be take to a login window. You can only login with your school account. Logging in with any email address that doesn't belong to the school's domain will result in a failed login attempt.

Is it safe to login? Are you collecting my passwords?

We DO NOT collect anyone's password. The login service is provided by the Google auth API. For more information please visit https://developers.google.com/identity/protocols/oauth2. The data we are storing is only your email address and your display name on the site.

Does the site admin know my true identity?

Yes, we do since we have your email address in our database, and your email address includes your real name. However, we are not affiliated with any party and we do not disclose or provide our database to anyone unless the data is needed by law enforcement.

How do I edit or delete my post or reply?

Unfortunatly we do not support users to edit or delete anything they have uploaded. We want everyone to be responsible and take accountability for every word. Think twice before you hit the submit button!

How do I report a post?

Copy the url of the post (jchannel.herokuapp.com/thread/THREAD_ID). Click on the three dots in the top right corner of the reply and copy the reply id to your clipboard. Email the url and reply id to jchannel2023@gmail.com and briefly describe the situation. Please report when the post has clearly violated our rules (Pornography, piracy, racism...). It may take a while to process the reports.

Community Rules

  • You will not upload, post, discuss, request, or link to anything that violates local or United States law.
  • You will immediately cease and not continue to access the site if you are under the age of 18.
  • You will not post any pornography, racism and pirate content. There are better places for those.
  • This community seeks high quality posts and any form of indecipherable text and gibberish text are not encouraged. 
  • No spamming or flooding. 
  • No impersonating a jchannel administrator.
  • The use of scrapers, bots, or other automated posting or downloading scripts is prohibited.
  • Violation of any of the above rules may result in a ban from voting, creating threads and replying to posts.
  • Do not reveal your irl identity unless you really want to do so.
  • For more details, please look at our terms of service.

For any questions, email jchannel2023@gmail.com
